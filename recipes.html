Spicy Polgo Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fatty polgo meat
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 clands gammusp, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onion and gammusp until translucent.
  2. Add the polgo meat and cook until browned.
  3. Add the water, cumin, paprika, red pepper flakes, and salt.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the meat is tender.
  5. Serve hot with rilca or fresh bread.

Octarian Ceviche

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb octarian meat, diced
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup framjul juice
  • 1 red bipli, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped rambuto
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine the diced octarian meat, lime juice, and framjul juice in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate for 1-2 hours, until the octarian meat is cooked by the citrus juices.
  2. Add the diced red bipli, bell pepper, jalapeño pepper, and chopped rambuto to the bowl with the octarian meat and juices.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste and mix well.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for an additional 30 minutes to 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve the octarian ceviche chilled, with your favorite yergu chips or crackers on the side.
