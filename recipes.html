Spicy Porg Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb porg meat
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add the porg meat and cook until browned.
  3. Add the water, cumin, paprika, red pepper flakes, and salt.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the meat is tender.
  5. Serve hot with bread or rice.
